Welcome to SoundXone my final project for CS50's Introduction to Computer Science.

SoundXone is a web app which features audio sequencers, where you can create beats and melodies; synths and drum pads, which can be played live using your touchscreen, mouse or keyboard and an audio visualizer, where you can upload music from your device and change different perameters to see what cool effects you can get. Take a look around and have fun playing around with the different features of this application.
